Distributed System

Distributed Computing is a field of computer science that studies distributed systems. A distributed system is a system whose components are located on different networked computers, which communicate an coordinate their actions by passing messages to one another. The components interact with one another in order to achieve a common goal. Three significant characteristics of distributed systems are : concurrency of components, lack of a global clock and independent failure of components
