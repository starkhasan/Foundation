Artificial Intelligence is an area of computer science that emphasizes the creation of intelligent machine that work and react like human. These processes include learning, reasoning and self-correction. The ability of a digital computer or computer-controlled robot to perform task commonly associate with intelligent begins.

Artificial Intelligence involve a variety of technologies and tools are as follows:-

  • Natural Language Generation :

    • It is a tool that produces text from the computer data. Currently used in customer service report generation and summarizing business intelligence

  • Speech Recognition:

    • Trascribes and transform human sppech into a format useful for computer applications. Presently used in interactive voice responce systems and mobile applications.

  • Machine Learning :

    • It provides algorithm APIs (Application Program interface) development and training toolkits, data as well as computing power to design train and deploy models into application, processes and other machines.

  • Deep Learning :

    • A special type of machine learning consisiting of artificial neural network with multiple abstraction layers. Currently used in pattern recognition and classification applications supported by very large data.

  • Biometrics :

    • Biometrics uses methods for unique recognition of humans based upon one or more intrisic physical or behavioral traits. Incomputer science particularly biomertics is used as a form of indentity access management and access control. It is also used ti indentify individuals in groups that are under surveillance.Currently used in market research.
