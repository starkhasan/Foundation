Manufacturing Science

Manufacturing science refers to the processes in which a raw material is converted into a useful product. It will certainly involve a number of stages, and each stage has a value addition to it. Thus an end product will have the highest value as compared to the starting raw materials. For example it will take a steel bar used in construction, it will have to undergo many processes starting from the iron ore mining, dressing, melting, casting, rolling, and finishing treatment. It has been seen that as the raw material progresses through each step in the manufacturing activity, it is processed from one stage to the other, and under goes value addition.
